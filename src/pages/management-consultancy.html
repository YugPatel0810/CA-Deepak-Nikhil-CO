The Essential Why

As businesses grow beyond day-to-day operations, financial decisions become more complex and consequential - yet many Indian SMEs and growing enterprises operate without the strategic financial leadership they genuinely need. A full-time Chief Financial Officer can cost ₹50,000 or more per month in salary alone, making it an expense most businesses cannot justify. Simultaneously, lenders now demand detailed CMA (Credit Monitoring Arrangement) reports before sanctioning working capital loans; acquirers and investors expect thorough due diligence before committing capital; and management teams require accurate business valuations for negotiations, restructuring, or exit planning. Without expert management consulting, businesses routinely leave tax efficiencies unexploited, fund flows unmanaged, and strategic opportunities unrealized.

Our Approach & Expertise

CA Deepak Nikhil & Co. offers holistic management consultancy designed to give growing businesses the financial intelligence they deserve. Our Virtual CFO service delivers CFO-level expertise on a flexible retainer - encompassing financial planning, budgeting, MIS reporting, cash flow forecasting, regulatory compliance, and investor relations at a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire. We prepare accurate, bank-accepted CMA Reports for working capital and term loan financing, conduct comprehensive due diligence for acquisitions and investments, and provide independent business evaluations for negotiations, disputes, or restructuring. Our Fundflow Management service ensures your working capital cycles are optimized and your financial obligations are consistently met. Strategic advisory engagements help leadership teams build financial strategies aligned with long-term business objectives.

The Tangible Benefit

Our management consultancy clients gain access to the financial foresight of a seasoned CFO without the overhead. They secure bank financing more efficiently with expertly prepared CMA reports, make informed investment and expansion decisions backed by rigorous due diligence, and manage cash flows with precision and confidence. The outcome is a business that is financially stronger, more investor-ready, and positioned for sustainable, profitable growth - with every major financial decision grounded in expert analysis rather than guesswork.
