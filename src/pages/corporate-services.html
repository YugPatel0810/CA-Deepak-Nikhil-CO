The Essential Why

Choosing the right legal and corporate structure at the outset is one of the most consequential decisions any entrepreneur or business owner makes. Whether you are incorporating a Private Limited Company, forming a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), or registering a branch, the structure you choose governs your tax exposure, personal liability, compliance burden, and ability to raise capital. In India, corporate formation and ongoing compliance are regulated by the Companies Act, 2013 and the LLP Act, 2008, administered by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). Errors in structure selection, defective documentation, delayed annual filings, or missed ROC returns attract penalties that can run into lakhs - and permanently impair the credibility of your entity with banks, investors, and regulators.

Our Approach & Expertise

CA Deepak Nikhil & Co. provides end-to-end corporate services - from entity formation to ongoing legal stewardship. We handle company registration, branch formation, and LLP incorporation through the MCA portal, advise on the most suitable structure for your specific business model and long-term goals, and draft legally sound LLP Agreements defining partner rights, duties, and profit-sharing ratios. Our company law compliance service covers annual returns, Form 8 and Form 11 filings for LLPS, ROC submissions, statutory register maintenance, and board and shareholder resolutions. We ensure your corporate entity is correctly structured, properly documented, and fully compliant - from the date of incorporation through every annual cycle thereafter.

The Tangible Benefit

Businesses structured with our guidance carry strong legal standing, protected personal liability, and full regulatory compliance from day one. Our clients avoid the most costly corporate pitfalls name rejections, documentation deficiencies, missed filing deadlines, and non-compliance penalties - and instead build a credible, well-governed corporate foundation that inspires confidence in banks, investors, vendors, and government authorities. When your company's compliance record is clean, every future transaction financing, expansion, or sale - proceeds on the strongest possible footing.
