Corporate
Services

End-to-end corporate structuring, from formation and regulatory compliance to ongoing legal stewardship.

The Essential Why

The legal structure you choose governs your tax exposure, personal liability, and ability to raise capital. Getting it right from day one — and maintaining compliance thereafter — is not optional under Indian law.

Our Approach & Expertise

We handle the complete corporate lifecycle — from entity formation and legal documentation to ongoing MCA compliance, annual return filings, and company law advisory — keeping your business structurally sound at every stage.

The Tangible Benefit

A properly formed and consistently compliant entity builds credibility with banks, investors, and government authorities — and protects the personal assets of every partner or director behind it.

Explore Sub-Services

Company Registration

End-to-end incorporation of Private or Public Limited Companies through the MCA portal — covering name approval, DIN, DSC, MOA, AOA, and the Certificate of Incorporation.

Branch Formation

Assistance with establishing a branch office of an existing company, handling all regulatory filings and documentation required under the Companies Act, 2013.

Company Law

Ongoing advisory and compliance support under the Companies Act, 2013 — covering statutory registers, board resolutions, directorship changes, and regulatory guidance as your company evolves.

LLP Formation

Complete registration of Limited Liability Partnerships under the LLP Act, 2008 — including DPIN, DSC, name reservation, FiLLiP filing, and LLP Agreement drafting.

Company Annual Returns

Timely preparation and filing of mandatory annual returns with the MCA — including Form AOC-4 and Form MGT-7 — keeping your company in good standing with the Registrar of Companies.

Company Management

Ongoing secretarial and compliance management — covering AGM support, board meeting documentation, statutory filings, and keeping all corporate records updated and audit-ready year-round.
