Audit &
Assurance

Rigorous financial examinations ensuring compliance, transparency, and stakeholder confidence across all verticals.

The Essential Why

Every registered company in India must undergo a statutory audit annually, regardless of turnover. Beyond the legal mandate, an independent audit builds the credibility that banks, investors, and regulators require before making decisions in your favour.

Our Approach & Expertise

We conduct rigorous, independent audit engagements across all categories — from statutory and tax audits to internal reviews and risk assessments — led entirely by ICAI-registered Chartered Accountants.

The Tangible Benefit

A clean audit opinion means stronger stakeholder confidence, better access to credit, and a financial record that holds up under any level of scrutiny.

Explore Sub-Services

Statutory Audit

Mandatory annual audit of your company's financial statements as required under the Companies Act, 2013 — verifying compliance with applicable accounting standards and Schedule III.

Internal Audit

A periodic review of your internal controls and governance systems — identifying risks and weaknesses before they become costly problems, with actionable recommendations for management.

Tax Audit

Mandatory audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act for businesses exceeding prescribed turnover thresholds — verifying the accuracy of your income, expenses, and deductions.

Risk Advisory

Identification and assessment of financial, operational, and compliance risks specific to your business, with practical guidance on controls and safeguards to reduce exposure.

Pre Audit

A preparatory review of your books and documentation before a formal audit — resolving discrepancies in advance so the actual audit proceeds smoothly without unwanted surprises.
