Accounting

Precision bookkeeping and comprehensive financial reporting tailored to keep your business fully compliant and informed.

The Essential Why

Every business operating in India is legally mandated to maintain accurate books of accounts under the Income Tax Act (Section 44AA), the Companies Act (Section 128), and the GST Act (Section 35). But compliance is only half the story. Your financial records are the backbone of every critical business decision - from controlling cash flow and securing bank financing to surviving tax scrutiny and planning for growth. India's regulatory environment is demanding: GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filings fall due every month, TDS must be deposited by the 7th, and PAN/TAN obligations carry strict timelines. Most GST notices and income tax penalties issued to Indian businesses arise not from fraud, but from poor records and missed deadlines. Clean, well-maintained books protect your business, your reputation, and your bottom line.

Our Approach & Expertise

At CA Deepak Nikhil & Co., we deliver far more than data entry. Our team manages the full accounting lifecycle - meticulous daily bookkeeping, GST compliance, TDS and TCS management, bank reconciliation, and periodic financial statement preparation. We handle TAN and PAN registration and assist with the drafting of Partner Deeds, LLP Deeds, and HUF Deeds to ensure your foundational documents are legally sound. Our accounting review service provides an independent check of your existing records, while our transitional advisory supports businesses undergoing structural changes, software migrations, or regulatory transitions. We leverage industry-leading accounting platforms to deliver accuracy, timeliness, and real-time financial visibility - so your books are always audit-ready.

The Tangible Benefit

Businesses that partner with us enjoy zero missed compliance deadlines, accurate Input Tax Credit (ITC) reconciliation, and financial statements that hold up under the scrutiny of auditors, banks, and the tax department. With your compliance calendar managed by experienced Chartered Accountants, you are free to redirect your energy from regulatory anxiety to what matters most running and growing your business.
